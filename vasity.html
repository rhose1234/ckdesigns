Varsityhub

A Free Marketplace, but for Students.

Varsityhub.ng is a marketplace where Nigerian students can buy and sell goods and services. Businesses on and around Nigerian campuses can advertise their goods and services on this free online platform. Its mission is to make students' lives easier.

Getting Set

My research effort began after a series of discussions with the client, with an emphasis on learning more about Nigerian campuses and online marketplaces.

Therefore, I was able to see what kind of style, color, and element would suit this niche well.
