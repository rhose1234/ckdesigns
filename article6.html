Have you ever felt this rush of adrenaline running through your body?

But being in love is not just something that can be associated with humans. People can fall in love with brands....Yhuppp, So why have people fallen head over heels for apple?

Apple is more than just a technology company. It's now a symbol of creativity and innovation.

Firstly, they've focused on creating an emotional connection with their customers Asides this, their products are designed with the 'User' at the center of innovation. They're always thinking about their users and how to give them a seamless experience with their product. Also, Apple has managed to create a strong brand image through their visuals in the mind of their customers. Hence, creating lasting memories and increasing their brand reputation. So People no longer buy from apple because of how good their products look... But, the special sense of attachment to an elite community, the feeling of belonging to something bigger

Recap:

To build a brand that your audience will love, Focus on these 3 things like Apple did.

✳ Foster emotional connection

✳ Put your Audience at the center of your product development

✳ Focus on building a strong image through your visual identity and campaigns.
