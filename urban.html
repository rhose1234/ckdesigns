As a newly launched real estate company, Urban Planet desired to break into Nigeria's saturated real estate market by providing exceptional services to their clients (especially middle-class and high-net-worth individuals).

A reliable leading go to source

I wanted to tell the company's story of becoming a go to source for quality homes/estates. However, in an exciting way that would build awareness and help Urban Planet stand out in the crowded real estate industry. I wanted the right perception to be shaped in their clients' minds.

I understood the company's needs perfectly. I needed to develop a logo & visual identity system that would consistently communicate the brand values and portray the right message to the public. In my research, I found that a geometric representation of the letter U and P to form an icon symbolizing a constructed building would be great for this project.
The reasoning here is the letter U and P would stand for the brand's initial name, and the geometric lines of the icon would suggest reliability, luxury and development. The icon is a key part of the identity system that is applied with other elements of the brand such as patterns and color

Logotype

Merriweather Serif : Chosen because of its pleasant and luxury feel

Merriweather Sans : Chosen to complement the serif. Has a timeless feel

Open Sans : Adopted because of its excellent legibility on print and web interface. Also pairs well with Merriweather Serif.
